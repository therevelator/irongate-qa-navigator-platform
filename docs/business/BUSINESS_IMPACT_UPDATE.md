### 📊 Key Features Implemented

#### 1. **Executive Dashboard**
- **4 Key Metrics at a Glance**:
  - Average Correlation Score
  - Total Revenue Impact
  - Average NPS (Customer Satisfaction)
  - Feature Adoption Rate

#### 2. **Key Insights Banner**
- **3 Strategic Insights Cards**:
  - Strong Positive Correlation findings
  - Customer Satisfaction impacts
  - Feature Adoption correlations
- Color-coded by impact level (Critical/High/Medium)

#### 3. **Interactive Correlation Matrix**
- **Scatter Plot Visualization**:
  - X-axis: Quality Score (60-100)
  - Y-axis: Revenue Impact ($K)
  - Z-axis: Correlation Strength (bubble size)
- **Color Coding**:
  - 🟢 Green: Strong correlation (>0.7)
  - 🔵 Blue: Moderate correlation (0.5-0.7)
  - 🟡 Yellow: Weak correlation (<0.5)
- **Interactive Tooltips**: Hover for detailed metrics

#### 4. **Historical Trends (12 Months)**
- **Multi-line Chart** showing:
  - Quality Score trend
  - Customer Satisfaction trend
  - Revenue trend ($K)
  - Churn Rate trend (%)
- **Dual Y-Axis** for different scales
- **Legend** for easy interpretation

#### 5. **Detailed Metric Analysis Cards**
- **For Each Quality Metric**:
  - Quality Score
  - Revenue Impact ($K)
  - Customer Satisfaction (NPS)
  - Feature Adoption Rate (%)
  - Correlation Strength

#### 6. **Expandable Deep-Dive Analysis**
- **Business Impact Insights**:
  - Revenue correlation calculations
  - Customer impact assessment
  - Adoption rate analysis
- **Strategic Recommendations**:
  - Prioritization guidance
  - Monitoring suggestions
  - Action items
- **What-If Scenarios**:
  - +20% quality improvement projection
  - -10% quality degradation warning
  - Revenue and NPS impact forecasts
