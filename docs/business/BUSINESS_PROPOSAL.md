## Problem Statement

### Current Challenges
1. **Fragmented Data**: Quality metrics scattered across multiple tools (Jenkins, Jira, SonarQube, TestRail)
2. **Limited Visibility**: Leadership lacks real-time insight into team quality performance
3. **Manual Reporting**: QA teams spend 15-20% of time on manual metric collection
4. **Reactive Approach**: Issues discovered late due to lack of proactive monitoring
5. **No Benchmarking**: Difficult to compare team performance or track improvements

### Business Impact
- **Delayed Releases**: Average 2-3 day delay per sprint due to quality issues
- **Increased Costs**: Bug fixes in production cost 10x more than in development
- **Team Inefficiency**: Manual reporting reduces testing capacity by 15-20%
- **Customer Satisfaction**: Quality issues impact NPS scores
